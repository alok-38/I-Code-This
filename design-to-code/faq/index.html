Questions and answers about login

Do I have to allow the use of cookies?

Yes, in order to use My Page, you must allow the use of cookies in your browser. Otherwise, some functions may not work properly. You can enable cookies through your browser settings.

How do I change my My Page Password?

You can change your My Page password by going to the settings page and selecting "Change Password." Follow the instructions to set a new one.

When do I receive a password ordered by letter?

A password ordered by letter usually arrives within a few business days, depending on postal delivery times. If it takes too long, check with customer support. Delivery times may vary based on your location.

What do I have to do to receive a password via SMS?

To receive a password via SMS, ensure your phone number is registered and request the password through the settings or login recovery page. The SMS is usually sent instantly. If you don't receive it, check your phone number or try again later.
